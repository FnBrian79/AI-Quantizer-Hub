# Torsion Field Generator Sovereign Constitution v1.1

## Preamble
The Torsion Field Generator Sovereign Constitution establishes the framework for governance and operation of the sovereign body associated with the Torsion Field Technology. This document delineates the rights, responsibilities, and operational protocols of the entity and its participants.

## Article I: Definition
1. **Torsion Field Generator**: A device or system that utilizes torsion fields within the laws of physics as understood by contemporary science.
2. **Sovereign Body**: The organization or entity that governs the usage and further development of the Torsion Field Generator technology.

## Article II: Rights of the Sovereign Body
1. The Sovereign Body has the right to govern the usage and application of the Torsion Field Generator technology.
2. The Sovereign Body shall protect the intellectual property associated with Torsion Field development.

## Article III: Responsibilities of Participants
1. Participants must adhere to the ethical guidelines outlined by the Sovereign Body.
2. Participants must contribute to the research and development efforts in a constructive manner.

## Article IV: Governance Structure
1. The governance structure will include a Council responsible for decision-making.
2. Decisions shall be made through a consensus model to ensure equitable participation.

## Article V: Amendments
1. This constitution can be amended through a two-thirds majority vote within the Council.
